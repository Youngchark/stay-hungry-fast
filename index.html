      INSIDE YOUR BODY
      How fasting works: ① Your body burns the sugar from your food first. ② Around 12h that sugar runs out, so it switches to burning your stored fat. ③ With no food coming in, it starts cleaning house — recycling old, damaged cells. The longer you go, the deeper the clean-out.

        The 72-hour payoff: you burn fat, your cells clear out damaged parts (autophagy), and around 72h your body recycles old/damaged immune cells — so when you refeed, stem cells help rebuild a fresher immune system.

        ⚠️ General education, not medical advice. A 3-day fast isn't safe for everyone (diabetes, pregnancy, eating-disorder history, certain meds). Drink water + electrolytes, and break the fast slowly to avoid refeeding syndrome. Sources: USC/Longo (Cell Stem Cell, 2014), fasting-physiology reviews.
